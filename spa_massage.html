3. Massage Therapy

Relieve tension, improve circulation, and melt away stress with our expert therapies.

Swedish Massage

Classic relaxation massage with long, flowing strokes to ease muscle tension and improve circulation. Perfect for stress relief and overall relaxation.

Duration: 60min | 90min

Deep Tissue Massage

Targets deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue to release chronic tension. Uses slower strokes and deeper pressure for specific problem areas.

Duration: 60min | 90min

Hot Stone Massage

Smooth, heated stones are placed on key points and used to massage the body. The heat helps muscles relax more deeply than with regular massage.

Duration: 90min

Aromatherapy Massage

Combines massage with essential oils selected for your needs (relaxation, energy, etc.). The scents enhance the therapeutic effects of the massage.

Duration: 60min | 90min
