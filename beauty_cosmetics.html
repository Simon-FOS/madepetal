3. Piercing & Body Art

Express yourself with our precision piercing services. We prioritize hygiene, safety, and artistry in every procedure.

  • Ear Piercing – Classic and curated placements for a stylish look.
  • Nose Piercing – Elegant studs and hoops for a bold or subtle statement.

  • Dermal Piercing – Unique single-point piercings for a striking effect.

Safe & Professional Piercings

All our piercing procedures are performed by certified professionals using sterile, single-use needles and high-quality jewelry.

  • Clean the piercing twice daily with saline solution
  • Avoid touching the piercing with unwashed hands
  • Don't remove jewelry during healing period
  • Avoid swimming pools and hot tubs
  • Watch for signs of infection (excessive redness, swelling, or discharge)

  • Earlobe: 6-8 weeks
  • Cartilage: 4-12 months
  • Nose: 2-4 months
  • Belly Button: 6-12 months
  • Dermal: 1-3 months
