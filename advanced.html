Our expert practitioners use the latest non-surgical techniques to help you achieve a youthful, radiant appearance with minimal downtime. At MadePetal, we combine medical expertise with artistic vision to deliver natural-looking results that enhance your unique beauty.

Our Advanced Aesthetic Services

1. Aesthetic Injectables

Precision treatments to smooth wrinkles, restore volume, and enhance facial contours.

  • Botox – Reduce fine lines and wrinkles for a refreshed look.
  • Dermal Fillers – Restore lost volume in cheeks, lips, and under-eye areas.
  • Lip Fillers – Enhance lip shape and volume for a natural, plump look.
  • Chin/Jaw Contouring – Define and sculpt the jawline for a more structured appearance.
  • Tear Trough Fillers – Minimize under-eye hollows and dark circles.

2. Microneedling & Skin Rejuvenation

Stimulate collagen production for smoother, firmer, and glowing skin.

  • Microneedling (with or without PRP) – Improves texture, scars, and fine lines.
  • Vampire Facial (PRP Facial) – Uses your own platelet-rich plasma for deep rejuvenation.
  • PDO Threads – Non-surgical facelift for lifting sagging skin on the face and neck.
  • Mesotherapy – Nutrient-rich injections for hydration and skin revitalization.
  • RF Microneedling – Combines microneedling with radiofrequency for enhanced tightening.

3. Laser & Light-Based Treatments

Advanced technology for hair removal, skin resurfacing, and pigmentation correction.

  • Laser Hair Removal – Permanent reduction for all body areas.
  • Laser Skin Resurfacing – Smooths wrinkles, acne scars, and uneven texture.
  • Laser Stretch Mark Removal – Minimizes the appearance of stretch marks.
  • Laser Tattoo Removal – Safe and effective fading of unwanted tattoos.
  • IPL Photofacial – Targets sunspots, redness, and uneven skin tone.

4. Skin Tightening & Lifting

Non-surgical alternatives to achieve a firmer, lifted appearance.

  • PDO Thread Lift (Face, Neck, Body) – Instant lifting with gradual collagen boost.
  • HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) – Deep skin tightening for a lifted effect.
  • RF Skin Tightening – Stimulates collagen with radiofrequency energy.

5. Pigmentation & Scar Treatments

Customized solutions to even out skin tone and improve scars.

  • Chemical Peels (Light, Medium, Deep) – Exfoliate and renew skin for a brighter complexion.
  • Stretch Mark Camouflage – Reduces visibility of stretch marks.
  • Scar Revision Treatments – Minimizes acne and surgical scars.
  • Hyperpigmentation Treatment – Corrects dark spots and melasma.

6. Non-Surgical Contouring

Sculpt and tone your body without surgery.

  • Cavitation (Fat Melting) – Targets stubborn fat with ultrasonic waves.
  • Radiofrequency Body Sculpting – Tightens skin while reducing fat.
  • Cryolipolysis (Fat Freezing) – Freezes fat cells for gradual elimination.
  • Vacuum Therapy (Butt Lift) – Enhances curves through suction technology.
  • EMSculpt – Builds muscle and burns fat with electromagnetic stimulation.
